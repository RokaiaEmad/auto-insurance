Who We Are

Students Building Real Solutions

We are a team of students working on a graduation project aimed at simplifying car insurance selection in Egypt. Our platform helps users compare insurance companies, prices, and coverage easily, making the decision-making process faster and more efficient.

We believe that every driver in Egypt deserves clear, honest, and accessible information — without the complexity or confusion that often comes with financial products.
