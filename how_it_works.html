The Process

4 Simple Steps

From search to policy — here's how our platform guides you.

1
🚗
Enter Your Car Details
Start by entering your car model, year, and the type of coverage you're looking for — comprehensive, third party, fire & theft, or personal accident. The more detail, the better we can match you.
2
🔍
Browse & Filter Results
Instantly see all 9 major Egyptian insurers ranked and filtered by price, rating, coverage type, and features. Use the sidebar to narrow results by roadside assistance, no-claim discount, medical cover, and more.
3
⚖️
Compare Side by Side
Select up to 4 companies and compare them in a detailed side-by-side table — covering rate percentages, claim timelines, max coverage limits, deductibles, and coverage categories — all at a glance.
4
✅
Choose & Get a Quote
Once you've found the policy that fits your needs and budget, click "View Details" to read the full terms, then "Get a Quote" to be connected directly with the insurer for the next step.
